How it works?

  1. Install the plugin/module on your high risk business WooCommerce or WHMCS website.
  2. Insert your own USDC Polygon (MATIC) in your WordPress wp-admin dashboard or your WHMCS admin dashboard.
  3. Save your settings and you are ready to instantly accept Credit/debit card (including MasterCard, Visa, and Maestro) – Apple Pay – Google Pay – SEPA or local bank transfer.
  4. When your customer place an order they will be redirected to pay through payment fiat onramp provider to make the payment using their credit card.
  5. A temporary wallet address is assigned to each order for enhanced privacy and precise payment detection.
  6. Infrastructure provider will handle your customer's instant ⚡ verification and any chargebacks. All payments are final and you will not receive any chargebacks.
  7. Verification of customer is instant ⚡ and it takes only 2 minutes!
  8. After your customer complete the payment though provider, order will be marked as paid automatically on your site and you get USDC instantly to your specified wallet address.
  9. For lost payments you can contact support or you can 📌 track payouts.
